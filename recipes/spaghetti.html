Shrimp Spaghetti With A Kick

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Zucchini
  • Chili Pepper
  • Shrimp
  • Garlic Herb Butter
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta. Bring salted water to a boil in large pot. Add spaghetti and stir occassionally until al dente. Drain and rinse while reserving a half cup of the pasta cooking water.
  2. Prep. Wash and dry produce. Cube the zucchini (quarter inch half moons). Thinly slice chili pepper. Rinse shrimp under cold water and pat dry.
  3. Cook Zucchini. Heat large drizzle of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add zucchini and chili pepper and stir occassionally until lightly browned.
  4. Cook Shrimp. Push zucchini mixture to one side of pan. Add large drizzle of olive oil to empty side and add shrimp. Stir/flip shrimp until opaque and cooked through.
  5. Toss Pasta. Add drained spaghetti, garlic herb butter, and half of parmesan to the shrimp mixture. Add reserved pasta cooking water in increments to toss everything together.
  6. Serve. Divide pasta between bowls. Top with remaining parmesan and drizzle of olive oil. Garnish with any chili pepper left over.
