Sweet Chili Pork Bowls

Ingredients

  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Yellow Onion
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Jasmine Rice
  • Peanuts
  • Ground Pork
  • Sweet Soy Glaze
  • Thai Chili Sauce

Steps

  1. Prep. Washing and dry produce. Thinly slice bell pepper and onion into strips. Quarter lime. Chop cilantro.
  2. Cook Rice. Combine rice, water, and pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce to simmer and cover for 18 minutes. Keep covered until ready to serve.
  3. Candy Peanuts. Heat pan over medium-high heat and add peanuts, water, and sugar. Cook and stir often until water has evaporated and the peanuts are coated. Turn off heat, transfer to plate, and wipe out the pan.
  4. Cook Veggies. Heat a large drizzle of oil in pan over medium-high heat. Add bell pepper, onion, salt and pepper. Cook until tender. Transfer to plate.
  5. Cook Pork. Add drizzle of oil to pan over medium-high heat. Add pork, salt and pepper. Press into even layer. Cook undisturbed until browned on bottom. Break up meat into pieces to finish cooking.
  6. Stir in cooked veggies, then add sweet soy glaze, thai chili sauce, and butter. Cook until evenly coated.
  7. Finish & Serve. Fluff rice with fork and stir in butter. Zest with lime to taste.
  8. Divide rice between bowls and trop with pork/veggies, peanuts, and cilantro. Serve with limes on the rim.
