Melty Monterey Jack Burgers

Ingredients

  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • Ground Beef
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Fry Seasoning
  • Monterey Jack Cheese
  • Potato Buns
  • Yellow Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Prep. Adjust rack to top position and preheat oven to 425 degrees. Wash and dry produce. Cut potatoes into half inch wedges. Thinly slice onion. Mince garlic.
  2. Roast Potatoes. Toss potatoes on a baking sheet with a drizzle of oil, fry seasoning, salt and pepper. Roast on top rack for 25 minutes.
  3. Make Onion Jam. Heat drizzle of oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until browned and softened.
  4. Stir in vinegar and sugar. Continue cooking until onion is coated and caramelized. Season with salt and pepper. Turn off heat, transfer to a plate, and wash out pan.
  5. Make Garlic Mayo. In small bowl, combine mayo, pinch of garlic, salt and pepper.
  6. Cook Patties. Form beef into two patties (use buns as guide for size) and season with salt and pepper. Heat a drizzle of oil over medium-high heat. Cook patties until browned.
  7. In the last minute or two of cooking patties, cover patties with Monterey Jack until cheese is melted.
  8. Finish & Serve. Halve buns and toast until golden. Spread buns with ketchup and garlic mayo. Transfer the patties to bottom bun and cover with onion jam. Top with bun and service with wedges on the side.
